Some common questions
were often asked

What is synthetic turf made of?

Synthetic turf is typically made of a variety of materials, including plastic polymers, rubber, and infull materials such as sand or rubber. The specific composition may vary depending on the manufacturer and purpose of the turf.

Is synthetic turf safe for pets and children?

Yes, synthetic turf is genrally safe for pets and children. It is designed to be non-toxic and free of harmful chemicals, and it is often used in playgrounds and other areas where safety is a concern. However, it is important to choose a quality product and ensure that it is properly installed and maintained to minimize any potential risks.

How long does synthetic turf last?

The lifespan of synthetic turf can vary depending on factors such as usage, maintenance, and climate. However, high-quality synthetic can last up to 20 years or more with proper care and maintenance.

Is synthetic turf environmentally friendly?

Synthetic turf can be more environmentally friendly than traditional grass, as it requires less water and maintenance, and eliminate the need for pesticides and fertilizers. However, it is important to choose a product that is made from recycled or sustainable materials, and to properly dispose of any materials at the end of its life cycle.

Can synthetic turf be installed over existing grass?

In most cases, it is necessary to remove existing grass or landscaping before installing synthetic turf. This allows for proper leveling and preparation of the area, as well as installation of drainage systems if necessary.

How much does synthetic turf cost?

The cost of synthetic turf can vary depending on factors such as the size of the area, type of turf, and complexity of the installation. However, synthetic turf is genrally more expensive than traditional grass, due to the cost of materials and installation.
